### Student

As a student on any of the many Micro Session hosted on This Is Code, 
you'll engage with your coach and other learners in an online classroom for lectures, 
discussions, and workshops. The length of a session is between 45 to 90 minutes. 
Some Micro Sessions spans over more than one online meeting.

**Note:** Enrollment opens up in January 2020. Stay tuned.
